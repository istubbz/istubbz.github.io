Guidelines for Coffee Club

    

 

  1. Dont talk about Coffee Club (obviously)
  2. Use the same measuring scoop & quantity of 4 scoops for all coffee when made
  3. This is a private club so take it in turn to bring in some coffee
  4. We collectivly look after the coffee pot & its state of cleanliness
  5. When a fresh brew has been made please message our members via the WhatsApp group
  6. Please supply a picture & description for the website (via WhatsApp grp is best)
  7. Do not deviate from the path, dont mix instant with ground coffee
  8. Discard coffee from the pot after 2 hours, to prevent a bitter taste
  9. Only coffee related chat on the WhatsApp group
  10. To join coffee club you need to bring in coffee in way of an application to join, you will then be added to the WhatsApp group
